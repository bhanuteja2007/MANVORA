Manvora Intelligence is founded and led by Bhanu Teja Modem — an AI Full-Stack Developer with a considered approach to digital craft. The practice sits at the intersection of machine learning engineering and refined web design, producing work that is as technically rigorous as it is visually precise.

Currently reading for a B.Tech in Computer Science at Vel Tech University (2024–2028), with a completed ML internship at Ediglobe sharpening production engineering instincts that go directly into client work.
